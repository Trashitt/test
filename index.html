Know Your Waste

Upload a photo of your waste for AI sorting, or search our quick disposal guide.

Scan Your Waste

Drop an image here or click to upload for instant AI classification

Wet / Organic Waste

Biodegradable waste like food scraps, fruit peels, meat, and coffee grounds.

How to dispose: Throw in the Green Bin. Can be composted at home or sent to municipal composting facilities.

Dry Waste

Non-biodegradable items that are not easily recyclable (e.g., multi-layer plastics, certain wrappers, rubber).

How to dispose: Clean and dry before throwing in the Blue Bin to avoid contaminating other waste.

Recyclable Waste

Clean rigid plastics, glass bottles, metal cans, and aluminum foil.

How to dispose: Rinse thoroughly to remove food residue. Place in designated recycling bins for material recovery.

Paper & Cardboard

Newspapers, cardboard boxes, office paper, and magazines.

How to dispose: Keep dry and flat. Throw in the Yellow Bin. Do NOT include oil-stained or food-soiled paper (like used pizza boxes).

Recycle & Repurpose

Understand how your waste gets a second life and find drop-off centers near you.

The Journey of a Plastic Bottle

1. Collection

Waste is picked up from your blue bins and transported to a recovery facility.

2. Sorting

Machines and humans separate plastics by type, color, and density.

3. Processing

Plastics are washed, shredded into flakes, and melted into tiny pellets.

4. Rebirth

Pellets are spun into polyester for clothes, shoes, or molded into new bottles.
